3–6 Month Outlook
Tesla in mid-2026 is effectively two companies priced as one: a mature EV automaker facing commoditisation pressure, and an AI & robotics platform in the early innings of autonomous transportation. The average analyst target of ~$407 now sits below the current price, implying the consensus views the stock as slightly stretched. The bull case ($600, Wedbush) rests on robotaxi and Optimus scaling as Musk envisions; the bear case points to slowing EV demand and a daunting 317x P/E. The next 3-6 months are a "show me" period — the market needs execution proof: more city launches, sustained margin improvement, and first disclosed robotaxi revenue. Key wildcards: FSD approval in China or a robotaxi safety incident could sharply re-rate the stock in either direction.
